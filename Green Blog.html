What I gained during class


LESSON 1: WHAT IS COMPUTING

• I now have an improved foundational understanding of the fundamental principles of computing. I'm not tech savy but the lesson was pretty beginner friendly at least in my experience.


LESSON 2: PROFESSIONS & CAREERS IN COMPUTING

• I think it is pretty cool to know that there are a lot of paths one can take in the field of computing. Kinda felt overwhelmed but I’m happy that there are a lot of possibilities and choices.


LESSON 3: INFORMATION PROCESSING CYCLE

• This lesson made me comprehend how information is processed in computing. The visuals were really helpful in putting it in my brain much more clearly.


LESSON 4: INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTER SYSTEMS

• This lesson gave me a comprehensive overview of computer systems, including hardware and software components, I now have a deeper understanding of how computers function which is pretty cool.


LESSON 5: NUMBER SYSTEMS

• This lesson gave me a refresh about the number systems used in computing, the systems are all so fascinating it all feels so organized.


LESSON 6: BASIC CONCEPT OF COMPUTER SECURITY

• This lesson made me recognize potential vulnerabilities of my computer security and now I employ more protective measures because people are quite creative in finding ways to harm your safety.


LESSON 7: PROTECTING YOURSELF AGAINST CYBER ATTACKS

• I now know more ways to mitigate the risks associated with cyber threats. Pretty scary to think that there are a lot of ways people can just take your private information without you knowing.


LESSON 8: THE INTERNET & WORLD WIDE WEB

• I now kinda understand the fundamental workings of the Internet and the World Wide Web, pretty cool to know how far we have come.


LESSON 9: BASIC HTML & CSS

• This lesson frustrated me but now I have foundational skills in HTML and CSS, now I can just create random sites for my own entertainment.


Highlight
